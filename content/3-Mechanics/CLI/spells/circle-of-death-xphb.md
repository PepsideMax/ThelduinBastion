# Circle of Death
*6th-level, Necromancy*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 150 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (the powder of a crushed black pearl worth 500+ GP)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Negative energy ripples out in a 60-foot-radius Sphere from a point you choose within range. Each creature in that area makes a Constitution saving throw, taking `dice:8d8|noform|noparens|avg` (`8d8`) Necrotic damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The damage increases by `dice:2d8|noform|noparens|avg|text(2d8)` for each spell slot level above 6.
